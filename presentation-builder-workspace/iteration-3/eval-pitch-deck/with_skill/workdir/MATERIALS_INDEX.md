## Key Facts Extracted

### Product
- Consumer fitness app
- Real-time form feedback on 40 strength exercises via phone camera
- On-device ML (no video leaves phone)
- Feedback: text + subtle audio (no screen-staring mid-rep)

### Target User
- Adults 25–45, lift at home or commercial gym 3-5x/week
- Know what to do, unsure if doing it right
- Don't want a trainer (cost), find async form-check subreddits inadequate

### Traction (9 weeks, closed beta / TestFlight)
- 340 active users
- 72% week-4 retention
- 38 min median session length
- NPS 61
- Top unsolicited feedback: "it caught the thing my trainer missed"

### Why Now
- MediaPipe Pose v3, Apple ARBodyTrackingConfiguration on A17+ — on-device pose estimation matured in 2025
- Post-Ozempic gym returners re-learning basics
- Garmin/Whoop normalized data-informed fitness; neither does real-time form

### Open Questions (from founder)
- Monetization model (subscription vs freemium — still testing)
- Expansion to mobility/yoga/cardio (planned but no clean story yet)
- Moat beyond on-device ML
